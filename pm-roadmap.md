## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Developers Transitioning to PM
1. **Leverage technical credibility** - Don't hide developer background, use it as strength với engineering teams
2. **Focus on "why" not "how"** - Train yourself to think problems, not solutions
3. **Get comfortable with ambiguity** - PM role is less structured than coding
4. **Learn to influence without authority** - You won't code it yourself anymore
5. **Develop business acumen** - Understand revenue, costs, unit economics

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Waiting too long to start applying (start at month 6-7)
- ❌ Focusing only on hard skills, neglecting soft skills
- ❌ Not building a portfolio (just resume isn't enough)
- ❌ Trying to learn everything (80/20 rule applies)
- ❌ Ignoring internal PM opportunities (easiest path)

### Accelerators
- ✅ Build in public (blog, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- ✅ Network early & consistently (1-2 coffees/week)
- ✅ Ship side project (even MVP shows execution)
- ✅ Get PM mentor (ask for 30-min monthly calls)
- ✅ Contribute to open source as PM (write specs, triage issues)
