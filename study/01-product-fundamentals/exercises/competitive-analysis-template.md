## Ví Dụ Hoàn Chỉnh: Food Delivery Vietnam

### Market: On-demand food delivery B2C tại Việt Nam

**Phân khúc:**
- Fast food / Quick Service Restaurants
- Local restaurants (quán ăn địa phương)
- Cloud kitchens / virtual restaurants
- Grocery + meal kit

### Competitors

| Loại | Tên | Mô Tả |
|------|-----|--------|
| Direct | GrabFood | Super app, market leader |
| Direct | ShopeeFood | E-commerce extension, aggressive pricing |
| Direct | Baemin | Korean-backed, strong branding |
| Indirect | Self-pickup (quán tự giao) | Giá rẻ hơn nhưng không tiện |

### Feature Comparison

| Feature | GrabFood | ShopeeFood | Baemin |
|---------|---------|-----------|--------|
| Real-time tracking | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Group order | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Scheduled order | ❌ | ⚠️ | ❌ |
| Multi-restaurant order | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| GrabRewards integration | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Flash deals | ✅ | ✅ | ⚠️ |
| Restaurant review system | ⚠️ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Corporate/B2B | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

### Pricing

| Competitor | Delivery Fee | Commission Model | Free Delivery? |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|---------------|
| GrabFood | 15k-25k VND | ~25-30% từ restaurant | GrabUnlimited subscription |
| ShopeeFood | 10k-20k VND | ~23-27% | Voucher campaigns |
| Baemin | 12k-22k VND | ~25% | First order, promotions |

### Positioning Map

```
          HIGH SERVICE QUALITY
               │
    GrabFood   │
   (premium,   │              (khoảng trống: quality + affordable)
    ecosystem) │
               │
───────────────┼──────────────────── AFFORDABLE PRICING
               │
               │    ShopeeFood
               │   (discount-heavy)
          LOW  │
```

**Opportunity gap:** Chất lượng service cao + giá phải chăng cho corporate/team orders

### Key Findings

**Điều tất cả làm tốt:**
- Real-time tracking
- Voucher/discount campaigns
- Restaurant variety

**Điều tất cả làm kém:**
- Corporate ordering (split bill, budgets, approval flows)
- Scheduled orders cho lunch meal prep
- Loyalty programs thực sự sticky (không phải chỉ discount)

**Top 2 Opportunity Gaps:**
1. **Corporate food ordering** — Không ai phục vụ tốt cho office teams order cùng nhau, chia bill, corporate budget management
2. **Subscription meal planning** — Không ai có tính năng plan bữa ăn tuần/tháng, tự động order theo schedule

**Differentiator tiềm năng:**
> "The only food delivery app built for teams, with shared carts, expense management, and scheduled meals."
